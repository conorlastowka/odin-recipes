Old Fashioned Dirt Cake

Dirt cake is a delicious & fun dessert that combines the great taste of oreos and pudding with the thrill of eating dirt

Ingredients

  • oreos
  • Vanilla Pudding
  • Gummy worms
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Grind Oreos up
  2. Prepare pudding
  3. Combine oroes and pudding
  4. serve in a flower pot with gummi worms
